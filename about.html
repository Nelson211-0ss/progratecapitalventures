Mission

Financial inclusion, engineered for how these businesses actually run

To revolutionise access to capital for women entrepreneurs in Uganda's informal sector by pairing accessible investment with the business support that makes it compound — and by proving the model returns capital reliably enough to scale.

Our vision

An economy where women entrepreneurs have equal access to financial resources and the opportunity to grow without asking permission from a system built to exclude them.

Our values

Integrity, innovation, inclusivity, and impact — the four tests every investment decision and partnership has to pass.
